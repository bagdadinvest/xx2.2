Why Turkey Has Become the Number 1 Destination for Cosmetic Surgeries

Turkey has rapidly become the top destination for cosmetic surgeries due to a combination of factors including advanced medical facilities, experienced doctors, and cost-effective treatments.

Historical Background of Istanbul: A Bridge of Continents

Istanbul, historically known as Byzantium and later Constantinople, is a city that straddles Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait. This unique position has made it a melting pot of cultures and a significant center of trade and commerce for centuries.

Accredited Hospitals and Experienced Doctors

Turkey boasts numerous Joint Commission International (JCI) accredited hospitals, ensuring high standards of healthcare. The country is also home to experienced doctors who are well-versed in the latest medical procedures.

Multilingual Health Consultants

To cater to international patients, Turkey offers multilingual health consultants who assist with communication and provide comprehensive support throughout the medical journey.

Cost-Effective Luxury

The devaluation of the local currency means that all the aforementioned luxuries are offered at much lower prices compared to other countries. This makes Turkey an attractive destination for those seeking high-quality medical care at affordable rates.
