BECAUSE YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO BE IN CONTROL

Inspiration for Article Intro Effects

by Henry James — Posted in Inspiration on May 21, 2014

Profile Management

Personal Information: Patients can view and update their personal details such as name, contact information, and medical history.

Medical Records: Secure upload and storage of medical records which can be shared with medical advisors.

Flight Reservations

Flight Search and Booking: Integration with flight booking systems similar to Google Flights, allowing patients to search for and book flights.

Flight Ticket Upload: Patients can upload flight tickets, which are scanned and extracted for relevant information (e.g., flight date, arrival hour).

Hotel Reservations

Interactive Map: Integration with Google Maps API to display pre-selected nearby hotels with pictures and details, similar to Google Hotels.

Hotel Booking: Option to reserve hotel rooms directly through the dashboard.

Medical Appointments

Appointment Scheduling: Patients can schedule appointments with medical professionals.

Appointment History: View past and upcoming appointments with details.

Transfer Requests

Request Transfers: Patients can request transportation services (e.g., airport pickup) through a chatbot.

Real-Time Updates: Live updates on transfer status once a driver is assigned.

Live Chat Assistance

Chatbot Support: A chatbot providing assistance with flight tickets, hotel reservations, and general inquiries.

Live Agent Chat: Option to chat with a live support agent for more complex issues.

Dashboard Overview

Reservation Summary: Overview of all current reservations (flights, hotels, transfers).

Notifications: Alerts and notifications for upcoming appointments, flight reminders, and other important updates.

Document Management

Upload Documents: Patients can upload various documents (e.g., ID, insurance).

Document Links: Links to uploaded documents stored securely.

Billing and Payments

Invoice Management: View and manage invoices for medical services, hotel stays, and other expenses.

Payment Processing: Secure payment gateways for handling transactions.

User Interface and Experience

Customizable Interface: Options to customize the dashboard layout and appearance.

User-Friendly Navigation: Intuitive design ensuring ease of use.
