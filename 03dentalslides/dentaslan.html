Dental Implants

A Long-Term Solution for Missing Teeth

Dental implants are a long-term solution for missing teeth, offering stability and durability. They involve placing a titanium post into the jawbone, which acts as a replacement root. This procedure provides a strong foundation for permanent or removable replacement teeth that are made to match your natural teeth. In Turkey, dental implants are performed using the latest technology, ensuring high success rates and patient satisfaction.
2

Dental Crowns

Restore Your Smile with Durable Crowns

Dental crowns are caps placed over damaged or decayed teeth to restore their shape, size, strength, and appearance. They are used to protect weak teeth, cover implants, and improve the look of misshapen or discolored teeth. Crowns can be made from various materials, including porcelain, ceramic, and metal. In Turkey, dental crowns are crafted with precision to ensure a perfect fit and natural appearance, helping you achieve a beautiful smile.
